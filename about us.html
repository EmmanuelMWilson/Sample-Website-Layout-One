About Emmanuel's Movie Theatre

It is an upcoming local theatre that allows the guests to reserve a spot where they can view their preffered films in a comfortable small environment without the whole drama that comes with normal cinema experience (such as overpriced tickets, long queues as well as overcrowded facilities such as the restrooms etc.)

It helps ease the stress by offering the following features:

  • A wide variety of films to select from.
  • Viewers get to enjoy their personal space while viewing.
  • Fewer number of viewers per session allowing for socialization after the session
  • Contact Us section for viewer's reservation and feedback.

Reservations can either be made online from the Contact Us page or as a physical visit to the viewing site using the directions also provided on the Contact Us page.
